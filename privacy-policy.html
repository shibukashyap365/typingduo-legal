⌨️ Typing Duo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

1. Overview

Typing Duo is an offline typing tutor for Hindi (Remington GAIL) and English. You don't need an account, and the app itself never asks for your name, phone number, email or any personal details.

2. Data stored on your device

Your lesson results, stars, streaks, theme choice and premium status are saved only in the app's private storage on your own phone. They are never uploaded to us. Uninstalling the app, or using Settings → Reset progress, deletes this data permanently.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version shows ads served by Google AdMob. Like all ad networks, Google may collect your device's Advertising ID, IP address, approximate (city-level) location and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads, as described in Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data. In the European Economic Area you will see a consent form first, and you can change your choice anytime in Settings → Ad privacy options. Buying Premium removes all ads, and with them this data collection.

4. Analytics (Google Firebase)

To understand which features are used and improve the app, Typing Duo uses Google Firebase Analytics. It automatically collects anonymous usage information: a random app-instance identifier, your device's Advertising ID, screen views, session events (for example that a lesson or speed test was completed, with its speed and accuracy numbers), in-app purchase events, device model and coarse (city-level) location derived from a masked IP address. This data does not identify you personally, is encrypted in transit, and is handled as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Purchases

Premium is a one-time purchase handled entirely by Google Play. We never see your card, UPI or bank details — the app only receives confirmation that the purchase succeeded so it can unlock Premium.

6. App permissions

Internet and network-state permissions are used only for ads, analytics and Google Play billing. Typing lessons, tests and your progress work fully offline.

7. Children

Typing Duo is a general-audience education app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page and inside the app with a new "last updated" date.
